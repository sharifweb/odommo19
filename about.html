About Us

Odommo '19 Youth Foundation is a youth-led organization registered by the Department of Youth Development, Bangladesh (Registration No: Mym-147/Gafar-006). Since 2018, it has primarily focused on Climate Action, Quality Education, and SRHR-related activities. The organization operates in four districts of the Mymensingh division, as well as in Kishoreganj, Rajshahi, and Dhaka. We emphasize capacity building for grassroots youth and raising public awareness.

Additionally, Odommo '19 also engages in emergency response work. Over the past 6 years, we have connected nearly 100,000 people across the country through around 60 different activities in various regions.
